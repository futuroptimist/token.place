Welcome to tokenplace!

tokenplace is a peer-to-peer generative AI platform that pairs those in need of LLM compute with individuals donating spare resources.

Heavily inspired by projects like Folding@Home, token.place aims to truly democratized AI by providing a completely free chat client and API. No strings attached, forever. Made possible by generous compute donors like you.

Access to and governance over these highly capable and sophisticated language models should be universal. It shouldn't belong to an elite few at the governmental, corporate, or individual level.

This project is completely open source (Github repository) and MIT licensed.
